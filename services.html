Payasam

Payasam is a traditional South Indian sweet dessert or porridge made by simmering milk, sugar or jaggery, and a carbohydrate base (like rice, vermicelli, dal, or tapioca). It is often flavored with cardamom and garnished with roasted dry fruits and nuts, and is deeply associated with festive celebrations and temple offerings.
