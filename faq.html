Frequently Asked Questions

1. What type of cuisine does Tamarind Tales serve?

Tamarind Tales specializes in authentic South Indian cuisine, featuring traditional dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and rich flavors.

2. What are your opening hours?

We are open every day from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

3. Do you offer vegetarian dishes?

Yes, we offer a wide variety of vegetarian dishes, including dosas, idlis, parottas, curries, and desserts.

4. Do you provide home delivery services?

Yes, we offer home delivery within selected locations. Please contact us for delivery availability.

5. Can I reserve a table in advance?

Yes, table reservations can be made by contacting our restaurant through phone or email.

6. Do you cater for special events and parties?

Absolutely! We provide catering services for birthdays, family gatherings, corporate events, and special occasions.

7. Are your dishes prepared fresh daily?

Yes, all our dishes are prepared fresh daily using high-quality ingredients and traditional recipes.

8. Do you have special dishes for children?

Yes, we offer a selection of mild and family-friendly dishes suitable for children.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit cards, credit cards, and popular digital payment methods.

10. Do you offer takeaway services?

Yes, customers can order food for takeaway and enjoy Tamarind Tales dishes at home.

11. Are there any signature dishes at Tamarind Tales?

Our signature dishes include traditional South Indian meals, crispy dosas, flavorful curries, and delicious desserts.

12. How can I contact Tamarind Tales?

You can reach us through our Contact page, phone number, or email for any inquiries or reservations.
